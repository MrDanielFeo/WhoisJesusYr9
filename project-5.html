The Ministry of Jesus

The ministry of Jesus covered: his teaching, parables, sermons, his miracles, his compassion and the love for his people. Above all he wanted all people to come to know him as the way the truth and the life. Refer to John 14:6 which says, Jesus says to him, "I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one comes to the father except through Me."
